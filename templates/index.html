About KANOON SARATHI

The goal of the project is to build a system tailored to the Indian Legal Environment which will facilitate the search and retrieval of information from distinct legal documents available in electronic formats. Leaving behind the traditional keyword match information retrieval, our system aims at dealing with the legal queries of the various stakeholders including lawyers, judges, civilians etc. using an ontology based querying mechanism.

This work is supported by the IHUB-ANUBHUTI-IIITD FOUNDATION set up under the NM-ICPS scheme of the Department of Science and Technology, India.
